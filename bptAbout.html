FOUNDER
Stephanie Cheng is on the board of a community health center that offers free and affordable healthcare to the medically underserved.

A native New Yorker, Cheng holds a Bachelors of Science degree in Business Management from Stony Brook University. Her background is in nonprofit work, marketing and operations.

She founded backpacktraveling.com to share her travel stories and passion for travel. Cheng is also the founder of streefoodnyc.com.
